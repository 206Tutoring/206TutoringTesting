I have kept in touch with several of my former students, as well as the evolution of the game over the last two and a half years.

I’m therefore happy to offer coaching services in a variety of formats, both live and online, for a reduced rate!

Online, I specialize in instructing low-stakes Multi-Table Tournament grinders. Live, I can give instruction in cash stakes up to $5/$10, and tournaments with buy-ins up to $1500. In both cases, I bring expertise in other areas of tournament poker, as well as extensive experience in playing other games and teaching other subjects, to the table.

My qualifications as a coach include:

- Experience coaching poker. This includes mentoring several 180 regs for free under twoplustwo.com’s old Small-Stakes MTT mentee / mentor program, most of whose games have drastically improved under me. I also used to instruct a stable of $2/180 and $12/180 professionals weekly.
