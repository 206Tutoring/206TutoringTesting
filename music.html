Music Lessons

206 Tutoring offers music lessons in piano, guitar, voice, violin, viola, drums, and more. If you’re interested in learning an instrument anew, or already have some expertise and want to pick up new techniques and styles, we have the right instructor for you.

We pride ourselves on hiring only the most skilled and dedicated music-education professionals, and matching students to someone aligned with their interests, aspirations, and ability level. Our instructors’ training runs the gamut from classical to rock, from jazz to pop—movie music and video-game music are particular specialties.

Our music lessons are fun, with a light-hearted approach puts you in in a better mindset to learn. All students of all ages and all levels of experience are welcome.

Our music lessons are flexible, with schedules and instruction much more individualized than you’ll find at a typical music academy. These are not your typical music lessons with rigid and grumpy professionals!

We will teach you what you want to play, how you want to play it.
